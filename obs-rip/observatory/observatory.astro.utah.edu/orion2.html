The Great Orion Nebula

This is a high resolution image of the Orion Nebula and the Running Man Nebula.

 

This is three combined exposures, one 10 min, one 5 min and one 2.5 min. A Canon Rebel XTi with a lens at 300 mm at f/5.6 and ISO 800 piggybacked on an pseudo-hand guided Meade 8" LX10
