Mud Spring point is to the North-east of Bryce Canyon, Utah. It is another potential site for the University of Utah's new observatory. The lights from nearby Ruby's Inn can be seen in the Mud Springs Milkyway photos which is just a mile from Bryce Canyon's gates. These Images were takein on Aug. 9th and 10th.
