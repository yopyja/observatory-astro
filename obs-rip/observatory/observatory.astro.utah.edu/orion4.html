The Great Orion Nebula

This is a high resolution image of the Orion Nebula taken in H-alpha wavelengths.

 

This is a composite of a 1200 sec (20 min), a 1320 sec (22 min), and a 2160 sec (36 min) exposures. A Canon Rebel XTi at ISO 200 with a Celestron 80mm refractor was used. It was mounted and tracked on a Meade 12" LX200 using a Meade DSI ProII for autoguiding. The image was taken by Paul and Cierra. Editing and processing by Paul.
