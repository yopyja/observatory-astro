Looking North on Mud Springs Point

Here is a picture looking north while on Mud Springs Point.

 

Looking at the horizon on the image, you may see a faint yellow glow. This is sky glow from light pollution almost 200 miles away in the Salt Lake and Provo cities. This is a great example of how bad our skies are becoming and how much energy is being wasted with extranious light sources.
