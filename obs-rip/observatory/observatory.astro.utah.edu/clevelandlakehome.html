Night Sky At Cleveland Lake

These images were taken at Cleveland Lake, Utah during a short vacation. The lake is about 8800 ft. This location is not as dark as the other mountains I have visited in Southern Utah.

Southern Facing Star Trails
One 26 min. exposure ISO 1600, f/4.5,
Canon Rebel XTi, Sigma 15-30mm @ 15 mm
The bright yellow/orange just to the top of the
trees is caused by light pollution from
nearby towns.
